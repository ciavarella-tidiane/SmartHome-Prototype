Stuffed Courgettes

Ingredients:

  • courgettes
  • cheese
  • minced garlic
  • fresh parsley

Step by Step:

  • 1. Prepare Courgettes: Remove and discard the giblets. Pat the Courgettes dry with paper towels and place it in a roasting pan.
  • 2. Prepare stuffing: Bring the water and butter to a boil. Stir in the stuffing mix, cover, remove from heat, and let rest for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, cook the celery and onion in butter. Add the cooked veggies to the stuffing. Stir in bread pieces, salt, and pepper.
  • 3. Stuff Courgettes: Stuff the bread mixture into the body and neck cavities of the Courgettes. Rub the skin with vegetable oil.
  • 4. Roast Courgettes: Loosely cover the bird with aluminum foil. Roast the stuffed Courgettes in a preheated oven until the thickest part of the thigh reaches 180 degrees F and the interior of the stuffing reaches 165 degrees F. Remove the foil during the last 30 minutes of roasting to brown the skin.
