Stuffed Turkey

Ingredients:

  • whole turkey
  • mild Italian sausage
  • minced garlic
  • fresh parsley

Step by Step:

  • 1. Season a turkey with salt and pepper.
  • 2. In a bowl, combine softened butter with dried thyme, rosemary, sage, garlic powder, and onion powder. Rub this herb-infused butter all over the turkey, inside and out.
  • 3. For the stuffing, cook Italian sausage in a skillet and mix it with sautéed onions, celery, carrots, garlic, stale bread cubes, parsley, sage, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  • 4. Moisten the stuffing with chicken or turkey broth. Stuff the turkey cavity with this mixture.
  • 5. Roast the turkey in a preheated oven at 325°F (163°C), following standard cooking times for the turkey's weight.
